Help Others

Every day I come up with a new idea. I think "oooh lets code that". Then I come across a problem. To do that I need a full on developmet team. Where do you get it? Where do you advertise "Come work on this with me- it'll be fun?". The answer is: Here. You can describe your project and people can volunteer to help you with it. Just remember to often check you notifications often.

Find things easily Just Click "Search"

Lets say that you are interested in creating a chat room by the likes of discord. Instead of scrolling through endless pleas for help, simply look at the top right corner of the screen, type your query, and tap "search". Immediatly filter through hundreds of requests and find just the ones you want.

How About Smaller Projects No Problem.

What if your project only needs one or two people? In that case, when you post a request, add a "people limit". This way, when you have all the help you want, the post deletes itself. No extra clicks or hassle needed.
